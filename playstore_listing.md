## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Build Your Network is the professional networking app designed for people who are serious about growing their careers, finding co-founders, discovering mentors, and building meaningful connections — not just collecting contacts.

**Swipe with purpose.**
Browse a curated feed of professionals in your field. Swipe right to connect, swipe left to pass. When both of you are interested, you match — and a conversation begins.

**Tell your real story.**
Your profile goes beyond a job title. Share what you're currently working on, what you're exploring, what you want to learn, and what you can offer. Find people who align with where you're going, not just where you've been.

**Built for every kind of networker.**
Whether you're a founder, freelancer, engineer, designer, investor, or job seeker — Build Your Network helps you find the right people at the right time.

✦ **Set your intent** — Are you looking to collaborate, find a mentor, hire, or simply explore? Let your intent guide who you meet.

✦ **Skills & Interests matching** — Get surfaced to professionals who share your skills and interests. No cold outreach into the void.

✦ **Priority Messages** — Send a highlighted message to someone you really want to connect with, even before they've swiped on you.

✦ **Real-time chat** — Once you match, start a conversation instantly. No friction, no waiting.

✦ **Active status** — Know who's recently active and ready to engage.

✦ **Verified profiles** — Optional professional verification so the people you meet are who they say they are.

- [ ] **Short description** — done (see above)
- [ ] **Full description** — done (see above)
- [ ] **Privacy Policy URL** — add before publishing
- [ ] **Content rating** — complete questionnaire in Play Console

### Before submitting build
- [ ] `app.json` versionCode is 1 for first release
- [ ] EAS build: `eas build -p android --profile production` → AAB
- [ ] Test APK first: `eas build -p android --profile preview` → APK
- [ ] Ensure `com.buildyournetwork.app` package name matches Play Console registration
- [ ] Sign release with production keystore (EAS manages this automatically)

### Play Console steps
1. Create app → select "App" type, "Free", language English
2. Upload AAB to Internal Testing first → share with 2–5 testers
3. Complete Store Listing (above content)
4. Complete Content Rating questionnaire
5. Add Privacy Policy URL
6. Complete App Content declarations (UGC, Ads = No, etc.)
7. Promote to Production → submit for review (~3–7 days for new apps)
